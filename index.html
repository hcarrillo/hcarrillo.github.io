Biography

Henry Carrillo is a Senior Computer Vision Scientist at Genius Sports and an Adjunct Professor at Pontificia Universidad Javeriana. His research interests are related to all forms of artificial intelligence applications, but particularly to computer vision, machine learning, data science, edge computing-IoT and robotics.

He is classified as an Investigador Asociado according to MINCIENCIAS latest evaluation (No 833 of 2018). In 2018, he was an International Visiting Fellow in the Institute for Analytics and Data Science (IADS), University of Essex (Colchester, UK).
